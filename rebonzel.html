about Tangled movie:

  • Directors: Directed by Nathan Greno and Byron Howard.
  • Voice Cast: Stars Mandy Moore as Rapunzel and Zachary Levi as Flynn Rider.
  • Animation Style: Features visually stunning animation blending hand-drawn and CGI techniques.
  • Musical Score: Composed by Alan Menken with catchy songs like "When Will My Life Begin?" and "I See the Light."
