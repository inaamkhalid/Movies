about Beauty and the Beast movie:

  • Live-Action Success: Beauty and the Beast (2017) is a live-action adaptation of the beloved animated classic, grossing over $1.2 billion worldwide.
  • Emma Watson as Belle: Emma Watson shines as Belle, bringing modernity and intelligence to the character.
  • Box Office Hit: The film was one of the highest-grossing of 2017, praised for its performances and faithfulness to the original.
  • Academy Award Recognition: Nominated for Best Costume Design and Best Production Design, highlighting its stunning visuals and design.
