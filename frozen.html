about Frozen movie:

  • Directed by Chris Buck and Jennifer Lee: The movie was co-directed by Chris Buck and Jennifer Lee.
  • Starring Kristen Bell and Idina Menzel: Kristen Bell voiced Anna, while Idina Menzel provided the voice for Elsa.
  • Memorable Music: The film features iconic songs like "Let It Go" and "Do You Want to Build a Snowman?" which became instant classics.
  • Box Office Success: Frozen was a massive commercial success, grossing over $1.2 billion worldwide and winning two Academy Awards.
